Welcome

I am an MSc student in Economics with an interdisciplinary background in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. My current research focuses on the energy transition, by adapting an IAM to include an energy-based production function and mineral constraints to the production of low-carbon energy.

Research Interests

  • Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs)
  • Energy transition and mineral constraints
  • Causal inference and impact evaluation
  • Ecologically Unequal Exchange
