Welcome

I am a MSc student in Environmental Economics with an interdisciplinary background in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. I am broadly interested in applying quantitative methods to post-growth questions around resource use, wellbeing, and inequality. In my current project , I am adapting an IAM of the energy transition to include an energy-based production function and mineral constraints.

Research

  • Energy transition and mineral constraints
  • Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs)
  • Causal inference and impact evaluation
  • Ecologically Unequal Exchange
