Welcome

I am an MSc student in Environmental Economics with an interdisciplinary background in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. I am interested in post-growth research, with an emphasis on ecological macroeconomic modeling. In my current research project I am adapting an existing integrated assessment model (IAM) of the energy transition, to include an energy-based production function and mineral constraints.

Research

  • Energy transition and mineral constraints
  • Integrated Assessment Modeling (IAMs)
  • Causal inference and impact evaluation
  • Ecologically Unequal Exchange
